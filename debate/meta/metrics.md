# Debate Process Metrics

Track these metrics weekly to improve signal quality and delivery speed.

## KPIs
- **Time to first acknowledgement** (target: <= 24h).
- **Time spent in ARGUE** (target median: <= 48h).
- **Arbitration rate** (high rate indicates unclear requirements/evidence quality issues).
- **Reopen rate of RESOLVED tickets** (quality indicator).
- **REJECTED ticket ratio** by category (`meta/rejection-taxonomy.md`).
- **SLA breach count** by priority level.
- **Audit completion rate** (target: 100% weekly).
